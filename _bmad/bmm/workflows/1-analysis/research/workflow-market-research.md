# Market Research Workflow
## ProjectFire: AI Virtual Creators Network

**Agent:** Business Analyst (bmm-analyst)  
**Module:** Business Modeling (BMM)  
**Output:** `docs/market-research.md`  
**Duration:** 60-90 minutes (deep research)

---

## Workflow Objective

Conduct comprehensive market research to validate the **ProjectFire** business model by analyzing:

1. **Competitive Landscape** - Who else is doing AI creators/influencers?
2. **Market Size & Growth** - How big is anime/cosplay content creator market?
3. **Customer Segment Analysis** - What do brands really want/pay for?
4. **Pricing & Revenue Benchmarks** - What's realistic pricing for AI creator content?
5. **Market Trends & Opportunities** - Where is this market heading?
6. **Distribution Channels** - How do AI creators get discovered/promoted?
7. **Barriers to Entry** - What makes this hard to do well?

---

## Research Methodology

This workflow uses a **five-layer analysis approach:**

### Layer 1: Competitive Landscape Analysis
**Goal:** Understand direct and indirect competitors

**Who competes with ProjectFire:**
- Direct: Other AI influencer networks (Nyla, Lil Miquela, Virtual humans)
- Indirect: Traditional anime cosplay influencers
- Indirect: AI content tools (Stable Diffusion, Midjourney used for similar purposes)
- Indirect: AI avatar companies (HeyGen, Synthesia - but different use case)

**Research Questions:**
- How many AI creator networks already exist?
- What platforms are they on?
- How many followers do they have?
- How are they monetizing?
- What makes them succeed or fail?

### Layer 2: Market Size & Growth Research
**Goal:** Understand total addressable market (TAM)

**Market Segments to Research:**
- Anime content creator market (followers, monetization)
- AI-generated content market (growth rate, adoption)
- Influencer marketing spend (how much brands pay)
- Social media growth by niche (anime/cosplay specifically)

**Research Questions:**
- How many anime fans on Instagram/TikTok globally?
- What's the growth rate of anime content consumption?
- How much do brands spend on anime influencer marketing?
- How big is the "digital creator" market?

### Layer 3: Customer Segment Analysis
**Goal:** Understand who will pay for ProjectFire creators

**Customer Segments:**
- Anime merchandise brands (Crunchyroll, Tokyo Otaku Mode, etc.)
- Gaming companies with anime aesthetic
- Fashion/cosplay brands
- Anime conventions and events
- Streaming platforms promoting anime

**Research Questions:**
- What are their marketing budgets?
- How do they currently source creator content?
- What pain points do they have?
- How much would they pay for AI creator partnerships?

### Layer 4: Pricing & Revenue Benchmark Research
**Goal:** Understand realistic pricing models

**Benchmarks to Research:**
- How much brands pay anime influencers (per post, per month, per campaign)
- Creator Fund payouts by platform (TikTok, YouTube, Instagram)
- Affiliate marketing commissions in anime/gaming niche
- Premium pricing for larger audiences

**Research Questions:**
- Micro-influencer (10K-100K): €200-€5K per post?
- Mid-tier (100K-500K): €1K-€25K per post?
- What affiliate programs exist in anime niche?
- Are brands willing to pay more for AI creators?

### Layer 5: Trends & Future Market Research
**Goal:** Understand where market is heading

**Trend Research:**
- Growth of AI in content creation (statistics)
- Acceptance of synthetic media by audiences
- Platform policies on AI content (TikTok, Instagram, YouTube)
- Future of influencer marketing (humans vs AI)

**Research Questions:**
- Is demand for AI creators growing?
- Are platforms restricting or enabling AI content?
- What's the future of the creator economy?
- Will AI creators become mainstream?

## Key Research Questions (Deep Dive)

### Question 1: Competitive Landscape
**"Who are the existing AI creator networks and what's their status?"**

**Specific Targets to Research:**
- Lil Miquela (Instagram, 2M+ followers) - success case study
- Nyla (AI influencer) - current status
- Other AI avatar creators
- Any anime-specific AI creators already out there

**What to Find:**
- How did they grow?
- How do they monetize?
- What's the engagement like?
- Are they still active/growing or declining?

### Question 2: Anime Creator Market
**"How big is the anime content creator space on social platforms?"**

**Specific Research:**
- Top anime cosplay hashtags (how many posts? growth trend?)
- Top anime fashion influencers (followers, engagement)
- Anime merchandise hashtags (engagement, content volume)
- TikTok anime trend data (views, growth)

**What to Find:**
- Is anime content oversaturated or underserved?
- What content performs best?
- What kinds of creators have loyal followings?

### Question 3: Brand Spending & Partnerships
**"What are brands paying for anime creator content?"**

**Specific Research:**
- Crunchyroll influencer partnerships (budgets, creator types)
- Anime brand partnerships (who partners with what creators)
- Creator marketplace rates (Billo, AspireIQ, HypeAuditor data)
- Anime convention sponsor spending

**What to Find:**
- What's the range (€500-€50K per post)?
- What factors determine pricing?
- Do brands care if creator is AI or human?

### Question 4: Platform Trends & Policies
**"Are social media platforms helping or hindering AI content?"**

**Specific Research:**
- TikTok policy on AI content (disclosure requirements?)
- Instagram Reels + AI (allowed? encouraged?)
- YouTube Polico on AI creators
- Platform bias (do algorithms favor/suppress AI content?)

**What to Find:**
- Is AI content allowed transparently?
- Will platforms restrict or encourage it?
- What are the compliance requirements?

### Question 5: Creator Fund & Affiliate Potential
**"Is Creator Fund + Affiliate realistic as primary revenue?"**

**Specific Research:**
- TikTok Creator Fund actual payouts (€/1K views in different regions)
- YouTube Partner Program rates (comparable)
- Anime product affiliate programs (commission rates)
- Creator earnings data (what do real creators actually make?)

**What to Find:**
- Is Creator Fund realistic for 60K followers?
- What's realistic affiliate income?
- Are there other passive revenue opportunities?
