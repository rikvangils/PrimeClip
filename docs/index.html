PeanutClip AutoFlow

Simple app page for TikTok app review

PeanutClip AutoFlow helps creators detect moments in longer videos, prepare short vertical clips, and publish approved content to social platforms.

Privacy Policy Terms of Service

What the app does

The app helps a creator or editor go from long-form source video to short clips that are ready for review and publishing.

Why TikTok is used

TikTok Login and the Content Posting API are used so a user can connect their TikTok account and publish approved videos from the app.

What data is handled

The app may process account identifiers, OAuth tokens, media files, captions, scheduling details, and publication status data needed to complete the publishing workflow.
