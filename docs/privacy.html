Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 19, 2026

PeanutClip AutoFlow respects your privacy. This page explains in plain language what data may be used, why it is used, and how you can contact us.

1. What this app does

PeanutClip AutoFlow helps creators and editors turn longer videos into short vertical clips and prepare or publish those clips to platforms such as TikTok.

2. Data we may collect

We may collect or process the following data when needed:

  • Basic account identifiers from connected services
  • OAuth access tokens and refresh tokens
  • Video files, thumbnails, captions, and related metadata
  • Publishing preferences, schedules, and status updates
  • Technical logs needed for security, debugging, and reliability

3. Why we use this data

  • To let you connect your TikTok account securely
  • To upload or schedule videos you have approved
  • To show publishing status and workflow results
  • To keep the service secure and working correctly

4. TikTok-specific data use

If you connect a TikTok account, the app uses TikTok OAuth and TikTok API permissions needed for the publishing workflow. TikTok tokens are used only to authenticate requests and perform actions you authorize.

5. How data is stored

Data is stored only as needed for the app to function. Reasonable technical and organizational measures are used to protect stored data. No security method is perfect, but we aim to reduce risk as much as reasonably possible.

6. Sharing of data

We do not sell your personal data. Data may be shared only with the service providers or platforms needed to deliver the workflow, such as TikTok, hosting providers, or infrastructure services.

7. Your choices

You can stop using the service at any time. You can also disconnect linked accounts and request questions about your data by contacting us.
